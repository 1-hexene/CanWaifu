## How to use?

1. Plug CanWaifu to anywhere of Can Bus. (But please ensure there's power supply.)
2. Connect your smartphone or other devices to WiFi CanWaifu.

> [!TIP]
> SSID: CanWaifu <br>
> Password: canwaifu

3. Open a web browser and go to http://10.0.0.1/
4. Enjoy
